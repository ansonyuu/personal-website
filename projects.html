Things I'm working on right now

Designing for reflect

  • Programming
  • UI/UX

reflect is a digitally mindful chrome and firefox extension that encourages users to reflect on their relationship with technology. Since November, I've had the chance to design the UI/UX flow, social media content, and brand identity.

Shadowing at AVA Technologies

  • Video Editing
  • UI/UX

AVA is a Vancouver based startup that makes the most beautiful IoT based indoor planters the world has seen (Not biased or anything). I had the chance to shadow the product, engineering, and marketing team. During my time, I led the transition from Squarespace to Shopify, photoshopped assets, and animated Lotties for the app.

Working on the organizing team of the Harvard Policy for the People Challenge

  • Politics
  • Design

Policy for the People is an online competition organized by college students around the world. On the (fully remote) team, I designed the logo, reorganized the internal workflow with Notion, helped make the website, and reached out to mentors.

Teaching debate at DSDC

  • Community
  • Leadership

Debate and Speech Development Community is an academy for young scholars with a focus on discussing important global issues and developing skills. Currently, I'm preparing the students from grades 4-7 for the World Scholars Cup

Things I've worked on in the past

Leading the SDC COVID-19 Relief Fund

  • Community
  • Leadership

The SDC COVID-19 Relief Fund was a joint effort between 10 organizations to provide PPE to senior homes. In the end, we were able to provide a total of 22,000 masks to 32 homes. I had the chance to help spearhead the initiative.

Creating decals for philanthropic restaurants
